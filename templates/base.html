Wanderphilly: Uncover Philly’s Hidden Gems, One Walk at a Time

About the Project

Wanderphilly is a platform designed to bring together the vibrant community of Philadelphia’s walkers and explorers. Inspired by our love for discovering hidden spots in the city, we realized that many local gems aren't easily accessible through traditional tourist guides. By enabling users to share and exchange their favorite walking routes, the platform fosters a sense of shared discovery while encouraging a healthy, sustainable way to explore Philly.

Guide

To use Wanderphilly, register and log in. Click the "Upload Route" icon to enter details like name, description, rating, and tags. Use the interactive map to create your route by clicking to add stops. Once finished, submit the form. To explore routes created by others, select the "View Routes" icon on the home screen. You can filter routes by rating, tags, and name in the top left corner.
